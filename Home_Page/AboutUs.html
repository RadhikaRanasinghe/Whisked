How it all started...

It was 1938 when my great grandmother Shoshanna fled war stricken Germany with nothing but the clothes on her back and a torn, hand written book that was coming apart at the edges. Through the years this was her most treasured possession as this book had been handed down through generations of the Friedrich family. It contained their most valuable secrets, the recipes to their beloved cakes.

Before the war her family were proud owners of the most celebrated bakery in Düsseldorf. People from all over the country came to taste the scrumptious cakes baked there. With these recipes in her hand Shoshanna felt invincible. Through her journey filled with endless trials and tribulations, Shoshanna finally arrived in Boston and with only her book in hand, she opened a small bakery. Soon this tiny bakery was filled with customers from everywhere waiting in line to taste these much talked about cakes.

And the name of that bakery?

Whisked!
